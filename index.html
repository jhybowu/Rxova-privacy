Privacy Policy  ·  Your trust is our priority

Overview

Rxova ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Rxova, you agree to the practices described here.

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the medication reminder service:

  • Medication data — names, dosages, schedules you enter.
  • Reminder logs — records of doses taken, skipped, or missed.
  • Device information — operating system, device type, and app version for diagnostics.
  • Notification preferences — your alert times and sound settings.

We do not collect your name, email, or any account information unless you voluntarily provide it for support purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver timely medication reminders.
  • To display your dose history and adherence statistics.
  • To improve app stability and fix bugs.
  • To respond to support requests you initiate.

We do not sell, rent, or share your health data with advertisers or third-party marketers.

Data Storage & Security

All medication data is stored locally on your device. If cloud backup is enabled in a future version, data will be encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256 encryption. We follow industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal or health information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process.
  • To protect the rights or safety of users or the public.
  • With service providers who operate strictly under our instructions and confidentiality agreements.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — view all data the app holds about you.
  • Delete — remove all your data at any time via Settings → Delete All Data.
  • Export — download your medication history as a PDF or CSV.
  • Opt out — disable any analytics or diagnostic data collection in Settings.

Children's Privacy

Rxova is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
